Detailed Description

The GeForce RTX® 4090 is the ultimate GeForce GPU. It brings an enormous leap in performance, efficiency, and AI-powered graphics. Experience ultra-high performance gaming, incredibly detailed virtual worlds, unprecedented productivity, and new ways to create. NVIDIA Ada Lovelace Architecture

Experience up to 2x performance in ray tracing and AI-powered graphics with the NVIDIA Ada Lovelace architecture. The new third-generation RT Cores deliver 2x ray tracing performance, while fourth-generation Tensor Cores with AI acceleration provide the foundation for NVIDIA DLSS 3.

DLSS 3

NVIDIA DLSS is groundbreaking AI rendering technology that increases graphics performance using dedicated Tensor Core AI processors on GeForce RTX GPUs. DLSS 3 uses AI to generate additional high-quality frames.
